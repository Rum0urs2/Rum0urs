Atmospheric Dynamics

Brewer-Dobson Circulation Analysis

Quantitative investigation of the stratospheric meridional overturning circulation using NASA's MERRA-2 reanalysis data. The project employs the Transformed Eulerian Mean (TEM) framework to diagnose wave-driven transport and residual mean flow. Analysis focuses on long-term trends in stratospheric mass flux and its implications for trace gas distribution and climate sensitivity.

analytics Technical Methodology MERRA-2, TEM Framework, Python
Machine Learning

LLM for Climate Science

Development of specialized Large Language Model (LLM) interfaces for interpreting complex climate datasets. By leveraging Google Earth Engine (GEE) multi-spectral datasets, the system facilitates natural language querying of geospatial anomalies and environmental trends. The architecture integrates vector embeddings of academic literature with real-time satellite observational data.

neurology View GEE Integration PyTorch, GEE, Transformers
Experimental Physics

Gas Cloud Chamber Construction

Design and fabrication of a high-precision diffusion cloud chamber for visualizing ionizing radiation and aerosol nucleation. The project involved thermodynamic calculations for temperature gradient stabilization and the integration of a Peltier-based cooling system. Used as a pedagogical and research tool to demonstrate particle tracks in a supersaturated vapor environment.
